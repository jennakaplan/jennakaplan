ABOUT ME

As a photographer, I express my feelings, ideas, and beliefs by following these wise words of this great documentary photographer. I enjoy experimenting in all fields of photography. THESIS

Revisions is a photo collage series that is intended to create this moment where our mind sheds a new vision of light on everyday images. The series is based off of one large photo collage and is supplemented by four small photo collages. Revision is the act of revising and editing, changing and modifying. The photo collages reveal the beauty of the world around us by changing and modifying the view of everyday images.
